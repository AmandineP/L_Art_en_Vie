The Festival L'Art en Vie
The festival Art in Life is a meeting between the fixed arts, such as painting , sculpture and installation art in motion and dance. This festival is one of a kind . It will take you to a world where Hiphop dance gives life to a contemporary sculpture and where classical dance gives emotion to a facility . Come immerse yourself in this world of dreams and beauty to escape , just a moment.
Works
Richard Stainthorp

Richard Stainthorp decided to use the wire to perform his works. A cold , hard material that his talent manages to turn into something you might expect endowed with life. According to him, the difficulty of working with such material only adds to its satisfaction when finishing a sculpture. His works express a succession of curves and movement. Many thanks emerges in , including through postures being adopted his characters.

www.stainthorp-sculpture.com

Dance Companies
The Australian Ballet

The Australian Ballet is to inspire, delight and challenge public through the power of performance. The Australian Ballet is one of the first world of ballet companies and has delivered extraordinary performance for over 50 years. A commitment to artistic excellence, a spirited style and a willingness to take risks have defined the company since its inception , both on stage and off.

www.australianballet.com.au
